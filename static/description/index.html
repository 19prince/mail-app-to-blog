Mailing to Blog

Publish your newsletters as blog posts with one click

Stop duplicating content. When you send a newsletter, push it to your blog as a draft post — ready for review and publishing.

Key Features

One-Click Publish

A "Publish to Blog" button appears on sent mailings. Click it to create a draft blog post instantly.

Draft First

Blog posts are created as unpublished drafts. Review and edit before making them live.

Choose Your Blog

Select which blog to publish to on a per-mailing basis via the Settings tab.

How It Works

  1. Send your mailing as usual
  2. Open the sent mailing and go to the Settings tab
  3. Select a target blog from the dropdown
  4. Click "Publish to Blog" in the header
  5. Review the draft post and publish when ready

Requirements

Requires Email Marketing (mass_mailing) and Blog (website_blog) modules.
